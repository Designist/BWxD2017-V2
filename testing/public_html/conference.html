Think with us

Think with us:

Every year, Better World by Design curates an engaging lineup of presenters, panelists, and workshop moderators. At every step of the conference planning process, the conventional conference structure of "speaker" + "listener" is reimagined and reworked to maximize interaction between content and attendee. Last year’s conference continued our tradition of bringing you great content in new ways. As lifelong leaders and learners, our presenters incorporate design thinking to create and refine bold and innovative ideas.
