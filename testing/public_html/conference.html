Think with us

Every year, Better World curates an engaging lineup of presenters, panelists, and workshop moderators. At every step of the conference planning process, the conventional conference structure of "speaker" + "listener" is reimagined and reworked to maximize interaction between content and attendee. As lifelong leaders and learners, our presenters incorporate and use design thinking to create and refine bold and innovative ideas.
